About us

Secure, Accessible, Intuitive.

We are VotingApp, an add-on to your current voting system that allows UOCAVA and print disabled voters to vote electronically from anywhere in the world. We make it simple, providing a superior experience for voters and election administrators.
Our Advantages

Superior experience for voters and election administrators.

  • Elegant, intuitive, and fully accessible.
  • Flexible marking options (mobile, tablet, and desktop).
  • Configurable to meet your jurisdiction’s needs.

Higher standard to build trust in elections

  • The only remote system to protect voter privacy.
  • End-to-end verifiable.
  • Observers can independently audit results.
