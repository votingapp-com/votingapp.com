Transparent and trusted mobile voting.

Election technology is outdated. VotingApp can be an integral part of making elections more secure and accessible. With VotingApp, you can cast your ballot from your smartphone at your convenience without sacrificing privacy or security.
SECURE

Protect your vote with leading device and data security

  • Security checks to ensure your device is free of malware.
  • Two factor authentication to ensure no one can vote for you except you.
  • Data encryption techniques that meet or exceed all industry standards.
  • Immutable data storage for your vote, that you can verify at any time.
ACCESSIBLE

Navigate the process easily with accessible and intuitive layout and design.

  • Adherence to the latest mobile accessibility standards ensuring an equitable voting experience for all.
  • Acceptance of user provided accessories meaning voters with disabilities don’t have to learn new technology to vote independently.
  • Voters with disabilities can vote using the native iOS and Android accessibility tools and screen readers, or they can use VotingApp's enhanced built-in accessibility features.
Transparent

Track your vote from beginning to end.

  • Before submission, view all selections, make changes, leave the app and return at a later time to finish your ballot.
  • After submission, verify your vote, as you marked it, has been submitted and counted.
  • Only you know your ballot selections, maintaining voter privacy.
Timeline

So when will I be able to vote from my smartphone?

Many private organizations such as local political parties and unions are using mobile voting already.  In terms of public elections, many states offer mobile voting alongside existing voting methods like in-person and mail voting, primarily for Uniformed and Overseas voters as well as voters with disabilities who wish to vote from home.

How can you prevent mobile voting from being hacked?
Today’s election technology is due for an upgrade.
  • Older computerized systems are likely to fail and are difficult to maintain.
  • Many current voting machines use outdated hardware and software that is no longer serviced.
  • Election officials know outdated voting machines should be replaced, but lack the funds.
That being said, security is a top priority for us. Our mobile voting system includes:
  • Multi-factor authentication that affirms the person voting is a real, registered voter.
  • Tamper proof read-only data storage ensuring data is not tampered with during the voting process or in transit.
  • Seamless integration with existing systems to prevent voters from voting more times than allowed.
  • Optional generation of printed marked paper ballots for tabulation, verification and auditing purposes.
+
Who benefits from mobile voting?
Everyone! VotingApp is a cost-effective and scalable voting method for jurisdictions. In addition, voters everywhere will be able to conveniently cast their ballots, without sacrificing security or privacy. Specifically, the Voting App improves the voting experience for:
  • Voters with disabilities - For many citizens with physical disabilities, voting in-person creates barriers to access and makes it difficult, if not impossible, to vote. Certain disabilities also render the act of hand-marking paper ballots from home inaccessible, and these citizens have been left with few options to exercise their right to vote secretly.
  • Military and overseas voters - People who put their lives on the line to protect our freedom deserve to cast their votes privately, conveniently, and securely. Currently, the majority of military and overseas voters rely on mail, email, or fax to receive and return their ballots. This is extremely cumbersome and oftentimes means sacrificing privacy.
  • Non-native English speakers - VotingApp offers visual and audio multilingual support, depending on the requirements of the jurisdiction, allowing voters of all backgrounds to vote independently and confidently.
+
Will mobile voting replace other voting methods?
VotingApp will expand voters’ options, not limit them. We believe mobile voting should be offered as an approved voting method alongside in-person and mail voting.
+
How is VotingApp different from other online voting solutions?
VotingApp is unique in that it has accessibility tools built directly into the app, giving voters with disabilities enhanced options in addition to the native iOS and Android accessibility tools.

VotingApp is also currently the only remote accessible voting system available for US public elections that provides complete end-to-end-verifiability, all while maintaining voter privacy throughout the entire election cycle. Voters, election officials, and election observers can be assured an election run on VotingApp is accurate, secure, and fully auditable.
