How does Voting App prevent someone from voting multiple times?
Voting¬†App creates a cryptographically unique signature for every voter that‚Äôs made up of two parts:

1) Registration information provided by the administering jurisdiction; something only you should know (I.e., driver's license number, street address, or SSN)
