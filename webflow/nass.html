Frequently Asked Questions

Ask us a question
+
Can VotingApp work with existing election systems?
Optimal for piloting, VotingApp works with existing Election Management Systems—this means that you can import  existing election information into our intuitive Admin Portal, or you can create an election from scratch.  

Voter Information can also be imported from an existing Voter Registration Database. You can add your entire voting population to the Admin Portal or simply limit it to a certain group of voters (such as UOCAVA voters).  

Once the election is finished, the information gets decrypted and you can tally the votes and create reports using our Admin Portal, or you can print out marked ballots which can be scanned into your existing Election Management System.
+
Can VotingApp produce a paper audit trail?
Yes, jurisdictions can insist on implementations that produce scannable marked paper ballots to be scanned into their existing standard Election Management System. They also have the added bonus that an individual ballot stored in our digital ballot box is the electronic equivalent of a hardcopy paper ballot.
+
How does VotingApp protect voter privacy?
Cryptographically unique signatures are created in an air gapped (not connected to the internet) system, so no one will be able to detect any personal information. A voters’ personal information is never stored on the blockchain, only the signature associated with the correct ballot style, so observers into the blockchain will not be able to see who voted.
+
How is VotingApp different from other online voting solutions?
VotingApp is unique in that it has accessibility tools built directly into the app, giving voters with disabilities enhanced options in addition to the native iOS and Android accessibility tools.

VotingApp is also currently the only system available for US public elections that allows for remote accessible voting while providing complete end-to-end-verifiability, all while maintaining voter privacy throughout the entire election cycle. Voters, election officials, and election observers can be assured that an election run on VotingApp is accurate, secure, and fully auditable.
